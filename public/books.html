Reading changed my life.

Every book below is one I’ve read that impacted my life.

I used to avoid reading as much as possible growing up. However, I trained myself to love reading as I grew older and realized the power of books.

Reading is a superpower. You can teach yourself how to do anything.

If you don’t know what to read that means you aren’t reading books. Virtually every book you read will have references and recommendations of other books to read. Start out by reading what you love.

“Read what you love until you love to read. Everybody I know who reads a lot loves to read, and they love to read because they read books that they loved. It’s a little bit of a catch-22, but you basically want to start off just reading wherever you are and then keep building up from there until reading becomes a habit. And then eventually, you will just get bored of the simple stuff.”
— Naval Ravikant

Elon Musk credits books with helping him achieve his success. When asked how he learned to build rockets, he famously replied, “I read books.”

When I read biographies the most common thing among the people I read about is that they all read books voraciously.

Make it a habit to read for 30 minutes per day. If you can’t do that start with one page per day.

Don’t have time? Rather than scrolling through social media open up your Kindle app and read for 2 minutes.
